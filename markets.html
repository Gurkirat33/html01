The Bustling Streets of Chandni Chowk

Famous Sections in Chandni Chowk:

  • Khari Baoli: Asia's largest spice market
  • Dariba Kalan: Famous for silver and gold jewelry
  • Kinari Bazaar: Wedding shopping paradise
  • Paranthe Wali Gali: Famous for traditional parathas
  • Bhagirath Palace: Electronics market

Street Shopping Guide

Sarojini Nagar Market

The Popular Sarojini Nagar Market

Shopping Tips:

  1. Visit early morning for best picks
  2. Bargaining is expected (start at 50% of quoted price)
  3. Carry cash for better deals
  4. Check clothes carefully before buying
  5. Be prepared for crowds

Shopping Hours & Tips

  • Best Shopping Hours: 11 AM to 7 PM
  • Weekly Offs: Most markets closed on Monday
  • Peak Hours: Evenings and weekends
  • Bargaining: Expected in local markets
  • Payment: Carry both cash and cards
