Cultural Heritage of Delhi

Delhi's culture is a beautiful amalgamation of various traditions, religions, and customs that have evolved over centuries. The city has been ruled by different dynasties, each leaving its unique mark on the cultural fabric of Delhi.

Religious Diversity

Major Religious Centers:

  • Jama Masjid: Largest mosque in India
  • Akshardham Temple: Modern Hindu temple complex
  • Gurudwara Bangla Sahib: Important Sikh shrine
  • Sacred Heart Cathedral: Roman Catholic church
  • Lotus Temple: Bahai House of Worship
