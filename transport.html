Delhi Metro

Delhi Metro - The Lifeline of the City

Metro Line Color Route Major Stations

Yellow Line Yellow Samaypur Badli - HUDA City Centre Chandni Chowk, Kashmere Gate
Red Line Red Rithala - Shaheed Sthal Kashmere Gate, Dilshad Garden

Metro Travel Tips:

  • Use Metro Smart Card for discounted fares
  • Avoid peak hours (9-11 AM, 5-7 PM)
  • Keep right on escalators
  • Security check is mandatory
  • Follow COVID-19 guidelines
