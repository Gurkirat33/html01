Traditional Delhi Cuisine

Mughlai Delicacies:

  • Biryani: Fragrant rice dish with meat/vegetables
  • Kebabs: Various types including Seekh, Shammi, Galouti
  • Nihari: Slow-cooked meat stew
  • Butter Chicken: Delhi's gift to world cuisine

Food Safety Tips

  • Choose popular and hygienic establishments
  • Drink bottled water only
  • Avoid raw foods from street vendors
  • Check reviews before visiting high-end restaurants
  • Carry basic medicines while trying street food
